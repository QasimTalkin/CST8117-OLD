Tic-Tac-Toe the Game

The game of tic-tak-toe is played in rounds. Players take turns selecting squares in the 3 by 3 grid which places their symbol of an X or an O in the box. A box can only be selected once. If a player manages to get a line of three of their symbols in a row (horizontally or diagonally) then they win the game. If all the squares are filled without a winner, the game is a tie and will simply reset.

Have fun!
